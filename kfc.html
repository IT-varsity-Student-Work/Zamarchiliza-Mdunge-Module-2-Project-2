K F C

KFC (short for Kentucky Fried Chicken) is an American fast food restaurant chain headquartered in Louisville, Kentucky, that specializes in fried chicken. It is the world's second-largest restaurant, with 22,621 locations globally in 150 countries as of December 2019. The chain is a subsidiary of Yum!
