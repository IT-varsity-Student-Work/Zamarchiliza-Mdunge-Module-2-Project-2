ITHALA BANK

As Ithala Development Finance Corporate(IDFC) we manage fully-integrated business activities that provide a range of financial and property services throughout KZN. Our mandate is to promote development within the province.Our primary development focus is to increase the participation of black people in all sectors of the economy, and positively impact on job creation, skills development and training. Ithala has three major wholly owned subsidiaries, namely Ithala Limited, the KZN Growth Fund Managers and Ubuciko Twines and Fabrics.
