MR PRICE

Mr Price, also known as MRP, is a publicly traded retail company based in South Africa, well known for its focus on value. The group retails apparel, as well as homeware and sportswear, through owned and franchised stores across 14 countries, primarily in Africa. It also services customers globally through its online stores. It operates under different store names, such as Mr Price Sports and Mr Price Home.
